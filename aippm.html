ALL INDIA POLITICAL PARTIES MEET

About AIPPM

"Think globally, act locally"
-Patrick Geddes

A place where global minds meet local affairs, a committee where notable representatives from all-parties gather to put forth their stance and come up with a solution to an impending problem.
AIPPM- All India Political Party Meet, a non-technical but powerful committee with all political parties of the nation. It gives you an insight into the Indian Constitution where people with different ideologies meet and approach effective solutions.
We at VBIT MUN are excited to announce All India Political Party Meet for VBIT MUN 2.0! Representing a diverse group of Indian politicians, debate and participate if you place patriotism above everything else!

Naman Vankdari

-Speaker

Naman Vankdari is a practicing advocate majorly practicing in the courts of Karnataka, pursuing his Master's in Law in OP Jindal University - Jindal Global Law School and also the Co Founder, Chief advisor and Senior Partner of a Youth based start up "The Indian Conclave". As an individual he is deeply passionate about promoting events that enhances capacity building and leadership and strongly advocates the fact that "true learning happens outside the 4 walls of the classroom". A seasoned MUNNer and a highly experienced Youth Parliamentarian who has a vast experience in simulating these sessions and training delegates in this forte strongly considers that these simulations promote nation building and helps the youth to develop patriotic consensus towards their nation.

Sathyajith MS

Deputy Speaker

Sathyajith MS is a fourth year law student pursuing his degree at University Law College, Bangalore University. He was also a Research Coordinator at Global Law Assembly. He has won International Law moot court competition in the very first attempt and followed it by a string of Best Parliamentarian awards in Bangalore and Hyderabad circuit respectively. He has also participated in Asian and British Parliamentary debates. He also writes columns for well known online media platforms. He finds tremendous passion in economics, history, constitutional law and political affairs. He is also a percussionist whose genre is mainly Hindustani Classical music.
